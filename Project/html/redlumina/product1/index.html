Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use RedLumina™?

1. Charge the Device
Before using RedLumina for the first time, charge the device. The indicator light will turn green when fully charged.

2. Prepare Your Skin
Cleanse your face and apply a conductive gel or water-based serum.

3. Power On
Press and hold the power button for 1 second.

4. Select Mode
Choose your preferred mode.

5. Adjust Intensity
Toggle between low, medium, and high intensity.

6. Treatment Application
Gently move the device over the under-eye area.

7. Power Off
Press and hold the power button for 1 second.

How long until I see results?

Within the first couple weeks, most users notice diminished dark circles, reduced puffiness & bags and an overall more glowing complexion.

After a few weeks of regular use, most users notice a further amplification of the previous effects as well as a reduction in the appearance of wrinkles & fine lines.

Does the red light damage my eyes?

In short, our product does not damage the eyes.

RedLumina uses red LED light therapy (light-emitting diodes) at a 630nm wavelength. This specific wavelength of red light penetrates to a depth of about 2mm, reaching the skin's deeper layers where collagen and elastin reside. It does not penetrate deep enough to reach or damage delicate eye structures like the retina.

It’s important to understand that different red light therapy (RLT) devices operate at different energy levels. High-energy RLT, like those used in medical settings, can pose a risk to the eyes and often require protective eyewear. RedLumina, however, uses low-energy red LED light, similar to what researchers have recently found to be beneficial in studies exploring RLT for dry eye and macular degeneration. That said, we're NOT advising anyone to look directly at the light, regardless of these recent findings.

Lastly, the design of our product ensures the light works on the under-eye area and does not directly target the eyes when worn correctly. You can also close your eyes while positioning the device for added peace of mind, and once in place, feel free to open them again. After all, the device is designed to target the skin in close proximity so the microcurrent can work its magic—our 2-in-1 feature that sets us apart from other brands!

How often should I use RedLumina™?

10 minutes of daily use is all you need to see results!

We recommend an initial course of 8 weeks followed by maintenance as required.

It is important to follow the recommended usage instructions and not to overuse the device.

Can I use it with other skincare products?

Yes, you can use RedLumina™ with other skincare products.

It is recommended to cleanse your face and apply any serums or creams before using the device.

You can also use RedLumina™ after applying skincare products to enhance their absorption and effectiveness.

Is RedLumina™ suitable for all skin types?

Yes, it is suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

It is a non-invasive treatment that does not use heat, lasers, or abrasive exfoliants, making it a gentle and safe option for all skin types.
