What is the Vital Breeze AC?

The Vital Breeze AC is a compact, portable air cooling device designed to provide personal cooling. It features adjustable settings and a rechargeable battery, making it suitable for use in various settings such as rooms, cars, camping, and offices.

How does the Vital Breeze AC work?

The Vital Breeze AC functions by drawing in warm air, passing it over a water-moistened pad, and then expelling cooler, moistened air. This process can help reduce the temperature in a localized area.

What are the key features of the Vital Breeze AC?

Affordable cooling, adjustable air conditioning, customizable settings, humidity regulation and energy efficiency.

How do I use the Vital Breeze AC?

Fill the water tank with clean water, turn on the device and select the desired cooling mode and adjust the airflow direction as needed.

Yes, its portable design and rechargeable battery make it suitable for outdoor activities like camping. However, it should be used in dry conditions to avoid damage.

You can empty and clean the water tank to prevent mold and mildew.​ make sure to wipe down the exterior with a soft, damp cloth.​ It’s good to periodically check and clean the cooling pad or filter as per the manufacturer's instructions.​
